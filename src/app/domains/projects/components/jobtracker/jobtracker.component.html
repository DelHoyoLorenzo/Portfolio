Job Tracker

"Job Tracker is an app crafted to simplify the tracking and logging of job applications. It lets users organize both standard and spontaneous job applications, while also managing files tied to the job-hunting process."

Features
  • .Centralized dashboard displaying all the job offers that users have applied to or are considering.
  • . Upload and store essential documents, including resumes, cover letters, recommendation letters, and any other relevant files.
  • . 100% Responsive App
