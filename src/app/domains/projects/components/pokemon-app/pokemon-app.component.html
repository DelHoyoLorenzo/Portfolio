Pokemon App

"An (SPA) application created with the goal of consolidating what was learned during the web development bootcamp at Henry, individually building the Frontend, Backend, and Database components".

Features
  • .Browse all the pokemons.
  • .Filter by type, origin, and alphabetical order. Sort by power.
  • .Create your own Pokemon with different types, stats, and names.
  • .View each Pokemon's details by clicking on the card.
